About

I’m currently a PhD researcher in the Datavysts Marie Skłodowska-Curie doctoral network, hosted at the Jožef Stefan Institute in Ljubljana, Slovenia. My project focuses on building trustworthy AI tools to increase political participation. This site is part blog, part lab notebook. You’ll find: Posts — essays on data, society, and politics.
