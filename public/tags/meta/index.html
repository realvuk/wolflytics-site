Why this notes section exists

I kept treating every post like an essay, so when the PhD got busy, the blog went quiet for months. This is the fix: a place for short, rough notes I can write in five minutes. A paper I read, a thought, something that surprised me. No pressure to be polished, that’s what the posts are for. If a few notes start clustering around the same idea, that’s usually the seed of a real post.
